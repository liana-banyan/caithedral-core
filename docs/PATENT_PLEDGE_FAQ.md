# Patent Pledge FAQ

**Cooperative Defensive Patent Pledge #2260**
**Liana Banyan Corporation · Wyoming · 50-year charter**

## What is the Pledge?

The Cooperative Defensive Patent Pledge #2260 is an irrevocable, non-weaponizable
commitment by Liana Banyan Corporation to:

1. Never assert the CAI™ Core patents against cooperatives or individuals for
   non-commercial use
2. Grant royalty-free patent licenses to any cooperative entity (per cooperative
   legal definition) for all use cases
3. Maintain the pledge binding through the Wyoming 50-year corporate charter
   (the charter dissipates to the cooperative at year 50)

## Who is exempt from patent licensing?

**Always free:**
- Cooperatives (credit unions, co-ops, worker-owned enterprises)
- Individual non-commercial users
- Academic and research use
- Educational institutions

## When is a commercial patent license required?

Commercial use by non-cooperative enterprises. See `LICENSE_SSPL.md` for the
full Commercial Use definition.

## Is the SSPL separate from the patent license?

Yes. SSPL governs **copyright** (source-code modification/distribution rights).
The Patent Pledge governs **patent** rights. Both apply to CAI™ Core.
